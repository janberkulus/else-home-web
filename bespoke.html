How It Works

From Idea
to Production

Our process is structured for clarity. You'll know where the project stands at every stage — with no guesswork and no surprises.

Typical Timeline

Prototype: 2–4 weeks from approval. Bulk production: 4–8 weeks from sample sign-off. Timelines vary by complexity and volume.

  1. 01
    Project Brief

    Submit your requirements via our enquiry form or by email. Include dimensions, intended use, quantity, and any reference materials. The more detail, the faster we can respond with accuracy.

  2. 02
    Technical Review

    Our production team reviews your request and prepares a clear production outline covering materials, construction method, component sources, and lead time.

  3. 03
    Material & Fabric Selection

    We present fabrics, fillings, and structural materials suited to your project. Physical swatches and material data sheets are available on request.

  4. 04
    Prototype & Approval

    We manufacture a physical prototype based on the approved details. You inspect, approve, or request adjustments before production begins.

  5. 05
    Production Run

    Production begins after prototype approval. Quality checks are carried out at multiple stages, and each unit follows the approved sample.

  6. 06
    Delivery

    We handle packing details, documentation, and freight coordination. We can deliver to your freight forwarder or directly to site.
