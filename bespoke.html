How It Works

From Brief
to Production

Our process is structured for clarity. You'll know exactly where the project stands at every stage — no ambiguity, no surprises.

Typical Timeline

Prototype: 2–4 weeks from brief sign-off. Bulk production: 4–8 weeks from prototype approval. Timelines vary by complexity and volume.

  1. 01
    Project Brief

    Submit your requirements via our enquiry form or by email. Include dimensions, intended use, quantity, and any reference materials. The more detail, the faster we can respond with accuracy.

  2. 02
    Technical Review

    Our production team reviews your brief and provides a detailed specification sheet — materials, construction method, component sources, and lead time estimate. We resolve any ambiguities at this stage.

  3. 03
    Material & Fabric Selection

    We provide a curated selection of fabrics, fillings, and structural materials relevant to your specification. Physical swatches and material data sheets available on request. You confirm your selections.

  4. 04
    Prototype & Approval

    We manufacture a physical prototype to your approved specification. You inspect, approve, or request adjustments. Production does not commence until you are satisfied with the sample.

  5. 05
    Production Run

    Full production commences on prototype approval. Quality checks at multiple stages. Progress updates provided on request. All units match the approved prototype specification.

  6. 06
    Export & Delivery

    Products are packed to export specification, palletised or container-loaded per your requirement. Full export documentation provided — commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin.
