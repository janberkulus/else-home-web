Our History

Built on Decades
of Manufacturing

Else Home was built from the factory floor up — not from a brand deck. Our understanding of bed construction, materials science, and export logistics comes from doing it, not designing around it.

  • 2008
    Founded in Turkey

    Established as a specialist bed base manufacturer serving the local hospitality sector.

  • 2012
    First Export Contracts

    Began supplying hotel groups in the Middle East and North Africa. First container-scale orders fulfilled.

  • 2016
    Mattress Production Added

    Expanded manufacturing to include pocket spring and memory foam mattresses. Integrated end-to-end sleep system supply.

  • 2020
    European Retail Partnerships

    Entered OEM and private label manufacturing for European retail groups. EN 1725 compliance achieved.

  • 2024
    40+ Market Presence

    Now serving over 40 export markets. 200,000+ units per year production capacity. Else Home brand established.

Quality Standards

Compliance & Certification

EN 1725:1998

European standard for domestic furniture — beds and mattresses. Structural and safety requirements.

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

All fabric and filling materials tested for harmful substances. Certificate available on request.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality management system certification. Consistent production processes and continuous improvement.

BS 7177:2008

UK standard for resistance to ignition. All foam and filling materials compliant for UK hospitality supply.
